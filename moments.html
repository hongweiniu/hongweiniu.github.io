These photographs preserve some of the moments that have mattered most in my life.

1994

1994年，我出生啦！

In 1994, I was born!

2015

2015年，本科毕业，与哈工大续约。

In 2015, I completed my bachelor's degree and renewed my journey with HIT.

2016

2016年，硕士期间赴UIUC访学交流，照片摄于Beckman Institute。这是我第一次来到UIUC；博士期间，我又以国家公派联合培养博士生的身份重访这里。

In 2016, during my master's studies, I visited UIUC for academic exchange. This photo was taken at the Beckman Institute. It was my first time at UIUC; during my PhD, I returned as a doctoral student in a government-sponsored joint training program.

2017

2017年，硕士毕业，再次与哈工大续约。

In 2017, I completed my master's degree and once again renewed my journey with HIT.

2022

2022年，博士毕业，终于成为一枚PhD。

In 2022, I completed my doctorate and finally became a freshly minted PhD.

2025

2025年，与爱人步入婚姻，开启人生的新阶段。

In 2025, I married the love of my life and began a new chapter.
