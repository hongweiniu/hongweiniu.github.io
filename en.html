About

Profile

Hongwei Niu, male, born in 1994, is a Han Chinese native of Hanggin Banner, Ordos, Inner Mongolia. He holds a Ph.D. and is an Associate Professor and master's supervisor at the School of New Energy, Inner Mongolia University of Technology. He has been selected as a high-level talent under the Inner Mongolia Autonomous Region's "Talent Development in Inner Mongolia" Program. His long-term research focuses on the computational simulation and design of new energy materials. He has served as principal investigator for research projects including the Young Scientists Fund (Category C) of the National Natural Science Foundation of China. As first author, he has published five SCI-indexed papers in journals including Physical Review Letters and Computational Materials Science.
